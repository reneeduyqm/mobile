Hobbies of mine

I started creative writing in my sophomore year of high school I do poetry and short stories. In addition to it, I like traveling and traveled to many places. I will write down differnet thoughs and stories which leads me to keep creative writing. This is one of the short stories' openning I wrote when I was in high school.

It is cold today, but clear, like all the darkness and dirt are cleaned up. “My child,” the sound was dry and hoarse, like a noise, “today, as the Memy,” she paused, “I will impart to you the tale of Christmas.” The Memy has a wizened face and a back slightly hunched. With each movement there was the creak of old bones. The old woman hunched over in her lounge chair, leaning closer to the fire, edging her hands towards the flames to warm them from the bitter evening. The light from the flames illuminated her tired, worn face, wrinkles boring deeply into her skin.

“It was also a cold day, but not clean at all…” All the memories flashed back, like she was back to the night, the cold and painful night she could never erase from her memory.
